Bass

I’ve tried for several years to getting some bass practice going. Last year, as the world came to a stop i thought that this would be the time to seriously invest in this hobby. For that, i researched what would be the best online course and ended up buying the Beginner to Badass.

2021-09-06

So far it’s been a nice course to follow along, i’m currently stuck trying to play Billie Jean, by Michael Jackson.

Basses

I currently have two basses:

Yamaha TRBX505 which i bought used. It is a 5 string model and has a toggle switch for active and passive modes. It is a lovely piece of gear.

Harley Benton. A cheap bass but i felt i needed a 4 string and more convencional instrument. It is solid and good looking.

Pedals

Zoom B3

AfterShock Bass Distortion

Both of these pedals enable a wide range of sounds. I could spend hours discovering and tinkering with them.
