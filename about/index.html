About me

2 minute read

I believe that a person should strive to better themselves everyday, even if it is just a small improvement. I’m currently trying to get my head around all sorts of app and game development, for many platforms. This site documents these efforts. Read on through all the sections to find out what i’m up to.

  • Sometimes i also stream my game development or just my gaming in general https://www.twitch.tv/phireheavyindustries

photography

You can often find me outside with one or more cameras around, trying to get that perfect shot. I commonly use:

Nikon D5100, with the 18-55 kit lens or the 35mm 1.8f lens

  • This is my main camera and the one that i most enjoy using. It’s not the most modern but it has enough features and quality and i’m not planning on getting rid of it anytime soon.

Sony DSC HX60V

  • This i purchased just before a work trip as i wanted something that was small and pocketable but still had manual controls for exposure and shutter speed. In that regard, the Sony works great, even if the wifi is unreliable and not very pratical to use.

Minolta SR-T 101

  • An analogue camera that is such a joy to use. I should use it more often but the weight of the thing puts me off using it more.

I usually post some of my photos to my Flickr.
